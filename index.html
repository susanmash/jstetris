TETRIS

Instructions

Press 'Start' button to begin game. You can press 'Pause' any time to pause the game.

Use keyboard arrows to move shapes. Click 'W' on keyboard to rotate shape clockwise or 'Q' to rotate counter clockwise.

Ten points added to score for each row that is cleared. See score display change colors when this happens :)

Enjoy!

Key Shortcuts

Press 'enter' to START.

Press 'shift' to PAUSE

Numpad keys can move shapes too! Eg; '4' to move left, '6' to move right, and '2' to drop down.
